Generate monthly time series of holdings by two-way sort procedure

Parameters:

  • stocks ‚Äì Stocks object for accessing stock returns and price data

  • label ‚Äì Name of signal to retrieve

  • signals ‚Äì Call to extract cross section of values for the signal

  • rebalbeg ‚Äì First rebalance date (YYYYMMDD)

  • rebalend ‚Äì Last holding date (YYYYMMDD)

  • pct ‚Äì Percentile breakpoints to sort high, medium and low buckets

  • window ‚Äì No. of months to look back for signal values; 0 is exact month

  • months ‚Äì Months (e.g. 6=June) to retrieve univ; empty for all months

  • maxdecile ‚Äì Include largest stocks decile from 1 through maxdecile

  • mincap ‚Äì Minimum market cap

  • minobs ‚Äì Minimum required sample size with non-missing signal values

  • leverage ‚Äì Multiplier for leverage or shorting

Returns

3-tuple of spread holdings, smb holdings, and subportfolio sizes

Notes:

  • Independent sort by median (NYSE) mkt cap and 30/70 (NYSE) HML percentiles

  • Subportfolios of the intersections are value-weighted;

  • Spread portfolios are equal-weighted of subportfolios

  • Portfolio are resorted every June; and other months‚Äô holdings are adjusted by monthly realized retx (i.e. dividends not reinvested)
